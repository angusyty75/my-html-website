Our Story

我們的故事

Founded in 2025, Digital Service Station started as a lean startup with AI with a simple mission: help local businesses succeed online by building practical, affordable digital products. Our small, focused team prioritises rapid prototyping, customer feedback, and delivering measurable results for small and medium-sized enterprises.

Over the past year we've partnered with early customers to validate ideas, iterate quickly, and deliver results—improving website performance, user experience, and business outcomes. We value speed, transparency and practical outcomes.

數碼服務站於2025年成立，作為一家利用AI精簡的新創公司，我們的使命很簡單：以實用且可負擔的數位產品， 協助本地企業在線上成功。團隊注重快速原型、用戶回饋，並為中小企業交付可衡量的成效。

過去一年，我們與早期客戶密切合作，驗證產品想法、快速迭代，並帶來實際成果 — 包括網站效能提升、 使用者體驗優化與實際業務成長。我們重視速度、透明與實用的成果導向。

Our History

我們的歷史

2025 - Founded as a Startup

2025年 - 新創團隊成立

Digital Service Station was founded in 2025 as a lean startup with AI focused on delivering practical digital products for local businesses. We began with a few pilot projects that validated our approach: rapid prototyping, tight customer feedback loops, and measurable business outcomes.

數碼服務站於2025年成立，利用AI作為一個精簡的新創團隊，致力為本地企業提供實用的數位產品。 我們以少量試點專案驗證方法，強調快速原型、緊密的客戶回饋循環，並交付可衡量的商業成果。

2025 - Early Customers & Iteration

2025年 - 早期客戶與快速迭代

During our first year we partnered with early customers to iterate on product ideas, improve website performance, and test marketing strategies. Those early wins helped us refine our offering and build case studies for future clients.

在第一年，我們與早期客戶合作，快速迭代產品想法，提升網站效能，並測試營銷策略。 這些早期成果幫助我們優化服務內容並建立未來案例。
